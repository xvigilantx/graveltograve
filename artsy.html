Three days. Six routes. Endless gravel.

This isn't about racing. It's about the moments between—the quiet stretches through bocage countryside, the unexpected climbs, the conversations over pizza at basecamp. It's about showing up with your bike and your crew, ready for whatever Normandy throws at you.

No support vehicles. No predetermined pace. Just maps, good company, and the understanding that the best stories happen when you're a little lost and a lot present.

Basecamp

Jullouville. Free camping under the stars. Wood-fired pizza oven. Hot tub for sore legs. Campfire for long conversations. Everything you need, nothing you don't.

The Routes

Mixed terrain. Some technical. Some flowing. All beautiful. Choose your own adventure each day. Regroup at cafés. Get lost together. Find your way back.

The Vibe

Relaxed but intentional. Coffee at dawn. Wheels rolling by 9. Back by late afternoon. Pizza by 7. Stories by the fire until whenever. Repeat.
